Our Story

JoinCare, a Web3 health management product with GameFi + SocialFi properties, is the first project in the metaverse to earn money by effectively living a healthy life. Users equip themselves with NFT in the form of AKSO cards, and earn tokens through regular work and rest and effective scientific sports. These tokens can be used in social scenes or exchanged for sports equipment, hardware testing instruments, upgrading NFT sports scenes, etc., and can also be withdrawn in the eco-supported digital currency exchange. The goal of JoinCare is to use GameFi's fun properties to empower millions of users to engage in scientifically effective health management and SocialFi's social properties to build a sustainable virtual scene + health content sharing platform.
